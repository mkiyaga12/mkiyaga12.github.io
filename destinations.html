Rwanda

The land of a thousand hills — where mist, mountains, and wildlife shape every journey.

Why Rwanda?

Rwanda is a place of quiet beauty — rolling hills, volcanic peaks, and forests alive with gorillas and golden monkeys. From the calm shores of Lake Kivu to the vibrant streets of Kigali, every corner carries a story.

  • Volcanoes National Park
  • Gorilla Trekking
  • Lake Kivu
  • Nyungwe Forest
  • Kigali City Life

Uganda

A land of dramatic contrasts — glacier peaks, deep lakes, and forests filled with birdsong.

Why Uganda?

Uganda is wild, diverse, and full of life. From the snow‑capped Rwenzori Mountains to the savannahs of Queen Elizabeth National Park, it offers some of East Africa’s most breathtaking landscapes and wildlife encounters.

  • Rwenzori Mountains
  • Queen Elizabeth National Park
  • Lake Bunyonyi
  • Murchison Falls
  • Kibale Forest
