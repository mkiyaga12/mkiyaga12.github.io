Explore the Beauty of Nature & Wildlife

From the misty peaks of the Rwenzori Mountains to the dense forests of Bwindi, I embark on journeys across Rwanda and Uganda to capture the incredible beauty of wildlife and landscapes.

Photography is more than just an art; it is a way to connect with nature and inspire others to cherish and protect our planet’s biodiversity. Whether it's the soulful eyes of a mountain gorilla or the golden hues of a sunset over Lake Kivu, every image tells a story worth sharing.

Browse through my travel experiences, photography collections, and personal stories, and join me on a journey to explore the wonders of the wild. Let's preserve and celebrate the beauty that surrounds us!
