Discover the Beauty of East Africa

Join me on a journey to explore the stunning landscapes and rich cultures of Rwanda and Uganda. Here are some of the must-visit destinations in these incredible countries.

Volcanoes National Park, Rwanda

Home to the famous mountain gorillas and spectacular volcanic scenery, this park is a nature lover’s dream.

Nyungwe National Park, Rwanda

Explore one of Africa’s oldest rainforests with its rich biodiversity, including chimpanzees and a canopy walkway.

Lake Kivu, Rwanda

A picturesque lake perfect for relaxing, boating, and enjoying the surrounding mountain views.

Bwindi Impenetrable National Park, Uganda

Famed for its mountain gorilla trekking, Bwindi offers breathtaking forests and unforgettable wildlife experiences.

Murchison Falls National Park, Uganda

Discover Uganda’s largest national park, where the Nile River surges through a narrow gorge creating the spectacular Murchison Falls.

Queen Elizabeth National Park, Uganda

A diverse park teeming with wildlife, including tree-climbing lions, and the breathtaking Kazinga Channel.
