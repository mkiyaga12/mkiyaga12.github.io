Welcome to my travel adventures!

Traveling through Rwanda and Uganda has taken me on unforgettable journeys, from the towering Rwenzori ranges to the heart of Bwindi’s dense forests. Each trip offers a new perspective on the rich biodiversity and cultural heritage of these regions.

Whether it's gorilla trekking in the misty mountains or discovering the playful antics of golden monkeys, every destination leaves me with new stories to capture. Hiking the Rwenzori Mountains is a personal favorite – the challenging trails, icy glaciers, and breathtaking views make it a photographer's paradise.

When I’m not chasing wildlife, I enjoy engaging with the local communities, learning about their traditions, and experiencing the warmth of their hospitality. Traveling isn't just about the places – it’s about the people and the moments that make the journey special.
